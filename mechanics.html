Mechanics

Before Gameplay

VibeCheck gameplay mechanics starts when a user clicks or taps any of these modes:

  • Easy
  • Medium
  • Hard

Gameplay

The Loading screen appears with a disk indicator based on the modes clicked by the users, the loading screen duration is 3 seconds long, then the game starts.

When the game has started, there are

  • Hints placeholder that gives clue to users
  • Hint button to reveal 1 letter/character
  • Music player

Points and Streaks

Users will gain points and streaks per correct answer, no points gain per incorrect answer. Every mode has 15 songs to answer and the goal is the get the highest amount of points and streaks as possible.

The more streaks users have, it means the user got the consecutive amount of correct answers. However, if they got the incorrect answer, the streak breaks and turns into peak streak. After the game ends, the peak streak converts into your all-time streak which saves into the leaderboards.

Each time user plays the game, points and peak streaks will accumulate to the leaderboards.

Modes

Each modes have different kinds of game reward system and gameplay experience:

  • Easy Mode:
    • 20 second duration
    • +100 points for correct answer
    • +50 points for correct answer in under 5 seconds
    • +20 per streak
    • +1 streak
    • +3 hints
  • Medium Mode:
    • 15 second duration
    • +200 points for correct answer
    • +75 points for correct answer in under 5 seconds
    • +25 per 2 streaks
    • +2 streaks
    • +2 hints
  • Hard Mode:
    • 10 second duration
    • +300 points for correct answer
    • +100 points for correct answer in under 5 seconds
    • +30 per 3 streaks
    • +3 streaks
    • +1 hint
